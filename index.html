About

Ratius Maximus is the "Exalted Rat of the Meme Empire" from Matt Furie's universe, famously known for his "Veni, Vidi, Squeaki" catchphrase. It is also the name of an Ethereum-based memecoin and a 2025 house music track.

Ratius Maximus features prominently as a satirical, emperor-style rodent in the creative works of Matt Furie (creator of Pepe the Frog).

Ratius Maximus is a satirical, Roman emperor-styled rodent character created by artist Matt Furie—the mastermind behind Pepe the Frog—who has officially transitioned into a prominent Web3 culture phenomenon with his own dedicated Ethereum-based ERC-20 memecoin.

Beyond the digital currency space, this distinct meme identity has also successfully crossed over into indie music culture, serving as the direct inspiration for a 2025 energetic house track produced by electronic artist BittraderX.
